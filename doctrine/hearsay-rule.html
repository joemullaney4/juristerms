Hearsay Rule

Course: Evidence

Hearsay Rule is a legal doctrine frequently discussed in law school and legal practice. Courts rely on the doctrine as a framework for analyzing specific legal disputes.

The purpose of the Hearsay Rule doctrine is to provide consistency in legal reasoning. By applying established doctrines, courts ensure that similar legal issues are treated in a predictable way.

Understanding Hearsay Rule is important for law students, attorneys, and judges because the doctrine often shapes how courts interpret statutes, evaluate evidence, and resolve disputes.
